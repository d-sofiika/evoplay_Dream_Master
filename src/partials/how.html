How to play

  • 1

    Match 3 or More Tiles – Swap adjacent tiles to create matches.

  • 2

    Use Boosters & Power-ups – Activate powerful effects to clear the board.

  • 3

    Complete Level Goals – Reach the target score or clear obstacles to win.

  • 4

    Earn Stars & Coins – Collect rewards to unlock new levels and features.

  • 5

    Compete with Friends – Play together and see who reaches the top first!
